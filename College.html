College

2012

All of these pieces were made for my Graphic Design and Communication A Level, which I attained a Grade A for my work. The ones shown are inspirational by James Dawe and Benjamin Savignac. I was very interested in photo collage and the mixture of texture and colour was interesting to play around and I thought the outcomes were very effective.

Savignac and Dawe both effectively and cleverly used the cut and paste method in their work along with digital programs to enhance them. Dawe has a very bold outlook to his illustrations and this makes them distinctive. Whereas Savignac gives reason in his pieces and this gives a different personal feel to evert piece. I was inspired to be creative in my work, for it not to always have a straight narrow path. For the pieces to be expressive and for everyone to see something different in them.
